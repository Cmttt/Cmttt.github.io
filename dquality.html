Data Quality

Completeness

The dataset comes from kaggle but is also scraped directly from the NBA.com/stats API. There are roughly 128,069 rows of data (total shots tracked). Roughly 5,500 of these rows contain null data and were excluded for the purposes of this project. As a result, the data includes information about 95% of the shots taken during the 2014-2015 season, a reasonably large sample size for testing and training.

Correctness & Coherency

Rows were spot checked to game logs and determined to be correct. Additionally, the information found during the initial scoping of the data was on par with expectations in terms of numbers as well as correlation. It would take too long to manually confirm each entry, but the numbers seem to be coherent and match with expectations.
